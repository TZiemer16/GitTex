Big fish\\
Reeled in with date-rape bait\\
bet you wholesome\\
clean the guts\\
best intentions\\
Travel down south\\
Land of nevergreen pines\\
Long and sharp\\
Big and tall\\
Love in a backwater boat\\
Sworn to the fish\\
Crucial to the bait\\
I am a fishing man\\
no tradition but the rifle,\\
and no kill with a rod\\
But I love to fish\\
This love you feel\\
when I date rape your mind\\
is custody complete\\
This lake is a prison,\\
bait is a bitch\\
keys to the catch,\\
Showing big rods and big love\\

% Andy Ziemer
% Sep 15, 2017
